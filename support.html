Need help with Stacked: Gaming Tracker? You’re in the right place.

Common Questions

  • How is my data stored?
    Your data is stored locally on your device and optionally synced via iCloud using CloudKit.
  • Can I use Stacked on multiple devices?
    Yes. With iCloud enabled, your data syncs automatically across iPhone, iPad, and Mac.
  • Does Stacked collect any personal data?
    No. Stacked does not collect analytics, advertising data, or personal information.
  • Can I add custom games?
    Absolutely. You can track any game you want, not just casino games.

Feature Requests & Feedback

Have an idea or found a bug? Feedback is always welcome.

Please contact the developer through the App Store listing using the App Support link.

Data Issues

If you experience missing or out-of-sync data:

  • Confirm iCloud is enabled for Stacked in device settings
  • Ensure you are signed into the same Apple ID on all devices

Responsible Use

Stacked is a tracking tool designed to help users stay informed and organized. It does not provide gambling advice or recommendations.
